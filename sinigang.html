Pork Sinigang

Ingridients:

  • Pork
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Sinigang Mix
  • Fish Sauce
  • Taro
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. First saute the onion until it caramelize a bit
  2. Then add the pork and cook until light brown
  3. Add the fish sauce then the water
  4. Put the Sinigang mix then after a while, add your veggies, and taro
  5. Cook for 10 more minutes and Enjoy!
